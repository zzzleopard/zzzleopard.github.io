What is an emergency kit?

An emergency kit is an kit ( or bag ) that have items in it, it's prepare for emergency. It will be helpful in natural disasters ( hurricanes, tornados, earthquakes ) or other bad conditions.

Why it is important?

Having an imergency kit was super important. It will take a few days to let the rescue supply get to the place where the disasters happened, which means that food, water and other items will stop for a few days, if you didn't have a emegency kit, you will get hungry and also dehydration. Some time you will cost a little bit for it. But trust me, it's worth it.

How to get one?

There are some kit that already packed and sell on the web, you can buy on the web or get these items on your own.

What should I buy?

Here is a list that may help you know what to buy:

  • food ( can food or snacks )

  • water ( 1 L per person each day )

  • first aid kit

  • flash lights

  • radio resever

  • electricity generator

  • tooth brush and tooth paste

  • tissue paper

  • emergency blanket

Other things
